Why "Syncing error 400" happens

Instapaper Offline downloads the text versions of your saved pages, which are provided by Instapaper. Instapaper has a limit on how many downloads can be made within a certain time, however. The limit varies based on how many articles you've downloaded recently (within the last hour or so).

Unless you're doing really heavy reading (and, hey, good for you if you are :D), this should only happen on your inital sync. The best advice I can give is to keep your Instapaper bookmarks well-culled ;)

For now, the most I can do is tell you to refresh in a bit. However, Instapaper will soon (in January) add an API for Reading articles, which may not have a connection limit, or one that is at least much shorter. Also, when full page support is added, this will be irrelevant, since downloads will not be going through Instapaper.
